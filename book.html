A Guide to Red River Gorge

"A Guide to Red River Gorge" is a book I created using Indesign. I love going hiking at the Gorge, and I wanted to create a guide to some of my favorite trails and restaurants, as well as provide useful information to both new and returning visitors. I illustrated the cover and each of the arches using watercolor and pen, then scanned them into the book.
